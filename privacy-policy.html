Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

SermonMate ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you create an account
  • Chat Messages: Messages you send to our AI chat feature for the purpose of providing personalized Bible guidance
  • Preferences: Language, theme, and app settings

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Usage Data: App features used, stories read, time spent in app
  • Purchase Information: Subscription status (processed through Apple/Google)

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our Bible study features
  • Power AI-driven chat responses with personalized guidance
  • Process subscription payments through App Store/Google Play
  • Send important app updates (with your consent)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the app

AI-Powered Features

SermonMate uses artificial intelligence (AI) to provide personalized Bible chat responses. Your chat messages are processed by our AI systems to generate relevant scripture-based guidance. We do not use your messages for advertising purposes.

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
  • Bookmarks and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • We retain chat history only as long as necessary to provide the service

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Google/OpenAI: AI chat processing
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: Payment processing

Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Deletion: Delete your account and data from within the app
  • Correction: Update your account information
  • Opt-out: Disable non-essential data collection in settings

Children's Privacy

SermonMate is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
