Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

SermonMate ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

  • App Preferences: Language, theme (dark/light mode), and settings
  • Bookmarks: Saved Bible verses and stories
  • Chat History: Your conversations with the AI chat feature
  • Reading Progress: Stories you've read

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version (for app compatibility)
  • Subscription Status: Processed securely through Apple App Store or Google Play Store

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Bible study features and personalized content
  • Power AI-driven chat responses for spiritual guidance
  • Process subscriptions through App Store/Google Play
  • Improve app performance and user experience

AI-Powered Features

SermonMate uses artificial intelligence (AI) to provide personalized Bible chat responses. Your chat messages are processed to generate relevant scripture-based guidance. Chat history is stored locally on your device only.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your payment information directly. All payment data is handled securely by Apple or Google.

Data Storage and Security

  • All personal data (bookmarks, preferences, chat history) is stored locally on your device
  • We do not maintain user accounts or login systems
  • Deleting the app removes all locally stored data

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: Subscription management (no personal data shared)
  • Google AI: Powers the AI chat feature
  • Apple/Google: Subscription payment processing

Your Rights

  • Data Control: All your data is on your device - you control it
  • Deletion: Uninstall the app to delete all data
  • No Account: No account creation required to use the app

Children's Privacy

SermonMate is suitable for all ages. We do not collect personal information from users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
